Ready to File?

Prepare with confidence.

Use this checklist to gather documents and complete required steps before filing.

Checklist

Gather These Documents

  • Recent pay stubs or proof of income
  • Tax returns from the last two years
  • Recent bank statements
  • A list of all assets and debts
  • Any lawsuits, judgments, or collection notices

Complete Credit Counseling

Before filing, the U.S. Bankruptcy Code requires you to complete a brief credit counseling course. This can usually be done online or by phone. Visit the U.S. Trustee's list of approved providers for the Virgin Islands to choose an agency. After filing, you'll also need a Debtor Education course before discharge.

Steps to Get Started

  • Gather your financial documents.
  • Complete the required credit counseling.
  • Consult with our attorney to prepare your bankruptcy petition.
